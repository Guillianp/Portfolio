About

Hola, I'm Guillian

I have always loved the process of creating something and seeing it come to life, which led me on this unconventional journey to a career in the tech field. In November 2021, I joined the Road to Hire coding apprenticeship, significantly changing my career trajectory.

I have pursued administrative roles in accounting and HR in the past four years while pursuing a marketing degree. Although vastly different than a tech career, I believe that all your experience compounds to build you into a better professional and individual.

As a continuous learner and a multi-passionate, through the apprenticeship, I have been able to expand my skill set to become a front-end web developer and combine my education, experience, and creativity to be a well-rounded tech professional.

Through the completion of the coding apprenticeship, I have been able to learn about the semantics and accessibility of HTML and CSS and expanded my ability to build more functional and practical websites with React and JavaScript.
